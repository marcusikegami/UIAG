Mark D. Scholl, M.D., P.C - Chief Medical Officer

Mark is an Orthopedic Sports Medicine Surgeon who trained at Northwestern University Medical School to receive his M.D., and the Emory University Department of Orthopedics for his orthopedic specialist training. He then completed a Fellowship in Arthroscopic Surgery and Sports Medicine at the University of Utah. He has been in practice taking care of complex joint injuries for over 15 years. Dr. Scholl, has an active orthopedic practice where he performs advanced techniques of joint repair and reconstruction, primarily of the knee and shoulder. He has special interest in cartilage preservation and maintenance of the biological surfaces of knee and shoulder, as well as restoration of joint stability and ligaments. Dr. Scholl primarily performs surgery arthroscopically or with other minimally invasive techniques, and enjoys developing improvements in repair options, and pioneering new procedures.
