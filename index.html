About Us

Injury Analysis Group specializes in providing Independent Medical Exams and Impairment Ratings. We have access to multiple expert physicians/surgeons across the country who can assist where applicable for your personal injury, medical malpractice as well as Worker’s Compensation cases.

Joe has been in the medical industry for over 30 years. His goal is to provide ancillary income opportunities to medical providers relating to endeavors to help patients and improve patient outcomes. Joe has recently built numerous dialysis clinics, micro-hospitals, medical clinics, an urgent care and surgery centers in multiple states. Joe has 8 years of experience in the personal injury space and is committed to help these vulnerable patients get the care they need.
